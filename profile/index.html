About Me & My Philosophy

I'm a Fulltime Learner and a graduate of a Vocational High School with a major in Computer and Network Engineer.

I really like computer technology, especially in the field of software engineering, because I see the impact of this field is tangible and revolutionary for aspects of our lives.

But that's not all. I view Software Engineering, or more accurately, Programming, as something truly fantastic and amazing. With it, I can imagine myself being able to do anything I want, like being in "god mode" in a video game, because I can do a series of things like:

  • Controlling and making everything according to the System or Logical Rules that I want.
  • Building and Experimenting with something interesting and challenging.
  • It challenges me to think outside the box and break the rules, but on the other hand it also teaches me to think computationally or logically.

For me doing all these things is very satisfying and interesting.

Why I Build Learning System

I am currently working and building my own learning system which aims to make all my goals achievable and solve my current problems.
I call this System Continuously Learning to Survive, because human brain is created and working to make human survive, therefore I think I can modified or manipulate my own brain to think in a way that Learning is the key to Survive.

The reason that drives me to build my own Learning System is because I see myself, especially when learning something, always feel less suited, not enjoying the learning system or the rules that are made, there is always a kind of feeling that I think in some part or step I can improve or change the way the Learning System is done, that's why I think I will start to create My Own Learning System to make me feel happy and enjoy, but also productive and have measurable results when learning.

Why I Choose SE as Career Path

As you can see, the reason I chose Software Engineer as my career path is because of my "passion" or interest, but that is not my only consideration when choosing this career or job, because I see that there are pragmatic opportunities in this field because our society is now very dependent on the Digital World, such as Education, Entrepreneurship, Health, Entertainment, and Others, so when I saw this, I thought this was the best combination between my personal interests and the opportunities that exist.
